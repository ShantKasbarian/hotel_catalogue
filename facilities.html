Our tennis court facility offers guests a convenient and relaxing place to play,
featuring a well-maintained clay court. The facility is located in the scenic area,
providing a beautiful backdrop for matches. Guests can get tennis equipment,
such as rackets and balls, directly from the hotel. Our tennis court also offers
private lessons with professional instructors. For added comfort, the facility includes
a lounge area, restrooms, and showers nearby.

Our hotel's bar facility provides a stylish and inviting space for guests to relax
and enjoy a wide selection of beverages. It also offers a range of cocktails,
fine wines, craft beers, and non-alcoholic options, tailored to various tastes.
The bar area is designed with comfortable seating, ambient lighting,
and a sophisticated atmosphere, making it a perfect spot for socializing
or unwinding after a long day.
